My Pet Lucy

What breed is she?

Let me tell you more about my pet Lucy. She is mixed breed of Toy Poodle and Maltese. She is hypoallergenic and great for people with allergies to pets.

What Food does she like?

  • Chicken

  • White Rice
  • Peaches
  • Apples

What Color is Lucy?

She was born with black fur, since her mother was black Poodle and her dad was white Maltese.After five months she strated changing her color to gray and almost white on her belly and paws. Now she is gray with white
